Introduction

Welcome to CodeUnity, a platfrom to connect you and other software engineering professionals, collaborating on projects and gaining valuable insight of the coding world. CodeUnity provides a secure enviroments for you to invite, and join projects from fellow developers.

Features

  1. User Profiles: Create your profile to highlight your key skills and intrests.
  2. Project Collaboration:Lead,create or join projects Collaborate with other software engineers. Could be just a fun mini project, or the start of something great!
  3. Linkedin Integration:Connect your linkden profile to CodeUnity to network.
  4. Coding Resources:Get your hands on resources provided from the CodeUnity team and from the community we have built on CodeUnity.
  5. Tips and ThreadsShare useful tips, experiences and insights on discussion threads. Here at CodeUnity we like to help you grow, and the way to do that is by giving back. Strengthen your knowlede, and understanding while you help others learn and succeed.
  6. CommunitiesWant to specialize in a certain field, or contribute to specific projects, join specialized communities. Create or join chats, confrence calls etc..
